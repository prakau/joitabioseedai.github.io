BioSynth Nano™

Precision Nano-Biological Crop Intervention

Tiny particles. Smarter timing. Stronger crops.

BioSynth Nano is JOITA's flagship nano-biological crop input platform. It is designed to support nutrient-use efficiency, foliar uptake, chlorophyll formation, and stress resilience when applied at the right crop stage.

Application: Foliar spray or seed-stage support, depending on crop, protocol, and validation status.

  • Nutrient-use efficiency
  • Foliar delivery
  • Chlorophyll support
  • Crop stress response
  • Biofortification research pathway
  • Lower input wastage through better timing

Best for vegetables, cereals, pulses, oilseeds, horticultural crops, protected cultivation, and FPO demonstration plots.

SmartSeedMat™

Biodegradable Seed and Nursery Support Material

A better start for stronger crops.

SmartSeedMat is a biodegradable seed and nursery support material designed to improve early crop establishment, moisture retention, and seedling survival under climate-stressed conditions.

Form: Flexible biodegradable sheet or mat concept based on plant-derived or bio-based material.

  • Germination support
  • Moisture retention
  • Root-zone establishment
  • Reduced transplanting stress
  • Weed pressure reduction pathway
  • Sustainable residue utilization

Ideal for nurseries, high-value vegetable farming, protected cultivation, reforestation, water-scarce regions, and FPO seedling units.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is JOITA Bioseed AI?

JOITA Bioseed AI is an agri-deep-tech company building nano-biological crop interventions, offline AI advisory tools, aquaculture support systems, and field validation platforms for climate-resilient agriculture.

What is nano-biological agriculture?

Nano-biological agriculture uses carefully designed nanoscale or nano-enabled biological systems to support better delivery, timing, and efficiency of crop inputs.

What is FarmAssist AI?

FarmAssist AI is JOITA's offline-first advisory system that helps farmers and FPOs decide what to apply, when to apply, and what risk to monitor.

Are JOITA products already commercial?

Some JOITA products are in pilot, validation, or early deployment stages. Product availability depends on crop, region, regulatory requirements, and validation status.

Can FPOs run JOITA pilots?

Yes. JOITA is designed for FPO-led demonstration pilots using lead farmers, crop-stage tracking, application guidance, and field feedback.

Does JOITA work only in India?

No. JOITA is India-led and is building international validation and partnership pathways, including Ghana/Africa and optional ASEAN pathways.

Is there a separate Malaysia website?

No. The current website is joitabioseedai.com. Malaysia-related information is integrated only as part of JOITA's optional ASEAN pathway.

Does JOITA promise fixed yield gains?

No. Crop response depends on crop, variety, soil, weather, timing, and management. JOITA focuses on field validation, correct timing, and data-backed adoption.

How can investors connect?

Investors and strategic partners can request the investor deck, data room, or pilot discussion through the Contact or Investors page.

How can students join?

Students can apply through the Join Us page for internships in AI, agronomy, nano-biotech, bioinformatics, software, content, and field validation.
