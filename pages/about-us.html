About JOITA BIOSEED AI: Transforming Indian Agriculture

Revolutionizing farming with nano-technology and AI solutions that increase yields by 40% while reducing costs by 50%

Our Mission: Sustainable Agricultural Innovation

JOITA BIOSEED AI was founded in 2023 in Kurukshetra, Haryana with a vision to revolutionize Indian agriculture through an integrated approach combining retail operations, advanced R&D in nano-technology, and AI-driven agricultural services. We identified the need for sustainable, innovative solutions that could make farming more productive, profitable, and environmentally friendly for India's 140+ million farmers.

Our Values: Blending Tradition with Technology

We believe in a holistic approach to agricultural innovation. By combining traditional farming wisdom with cutting-edge technology, we create sustainable solutions that benefit farmers, consumers, and the environment. Our products are designed to work with nature, not against it, while ensuring regulatory compliance and ethical practices in AI development. Our solutions have been proven to increase crop yields by up to 40% while reducing input costs by 50%.

Our Approach: Integrated Agricultural Solutions

Through our three-pronged approach: We operate a retail business providing quality agricultural inputs, conduct R&D to develop innovative solutions like DaturaGuard Nano and RootBoost Nano, and leverage AI technology with our PestShield AI app to provide real-time crop management insights. This integrated approach ensures comprehensive support for modern farming needs across different agro-climatic zones in India.
