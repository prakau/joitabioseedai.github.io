About JOITA BIOSEED AI

Revolutionizing Indian Agriculture with Innovation and Care

Our Story

JOITA BIOSEED AI Pvt Ltd was founded with a vision to revolutionize Indian agriculture through an integrated approach combining retail operations, advanced R&D in nano-technology, and AI-driven agricultural services. We identified the need for sustainable, innovative solutions that could make farming more productive, profitable, and environmentally friendly.

What We Believe

We believe in a holistic approach to agricultural innovation. By combining traditional farming wisdom with cutting-edge technology, we can create sustainable solutions that benefit farmers, consumers, and the environment while ensuring regulatory compliance and ethical practices in AI development.

How We Do It

Through our three-pronged approach: We operate a retail business providing quality agricultural inputs, conduct R&D to develop innovative solutions like DaturaGuard Nano and RootBoost Nano, and leverage AI technology with our PestShield AI app to provide real-time crop management insights. This integrated approach ensures comprehensive support for modern farming needs.
