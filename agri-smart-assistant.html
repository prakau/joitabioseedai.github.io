The Agri-Smart Assistant is JOITA's public FarmAssist AI experience. It is designed for farmers, FPOs, field workers, and institutional pilots that need practical crop-stage guidance through a public, offline-first assistant.

Crop Advisory

Ask questions about crops, fertilization, pest monitoring, disease risk, and stage-wise management.
